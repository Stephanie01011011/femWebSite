About Us

Learn all about Elect Women!

Elect Women! was founded January 2020, in Indianapolis. They are a small team dedicated to research of womens issues and gathering as much information in one place for all feminists to donate and engage in political action. Elect Women! prides themselves on bringing nothng but plain, hard facts to this website, to stop the spread of misinformation to the feminist community. Elect Women! welcomes people of all genders/ non binary peoples, as well as all ages.

How we've helped women in our community

Elect Women! has donated over $350,000 to various causes that support women! We use all proceeds from ads on our site to donate to the charities we have listed in our charities section. We encourage all of our followers and supporters to find local womens charities and organizations and helping out in any way possible. Volunteer time at a womens domestic abuse facility, or donate products like pads/ tampons, socks, underwear, and jackets.
